About Us

Strawberry Creek Design Center is a community of creative businesses: architects, artists, craftspeople, solopreneurs, movie & and video producers, graphic designers, multimedia and software developers, and other innovative startups.
The office is operated as a shared collective. Share the space with a fun mix of folks.
We are open to accomodating your needs: from an affordable cubicle to a private office for a growing team.
