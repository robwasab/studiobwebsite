About Studio B

Tucked just off Strawberry Park in Berkeley, Studio B Co-Working is an intimate, design-forward co-working cooperative for people who care about where and how they work.

The creek runs literally 15 feet from our front door and conference room. With the windows open, you can hear it gurgling.

Step outside and you’re in Strawberry Park. The Hidden Café is right there. Grab a table or chair and set up anywhere in the park. Sometimes the best conference room has trees for walls. Hold walking meetings. Take a client to the grass. Work under a tree. Let your kids play on the playground after school.

And when you’re ready to focus, come back inside to a calm, beautiful, professional space.

Why Studio B Feels Different

This is not a high-volume coworking warehouse. This is a small, curated community of 8–10 thoughtful professionals; film makers, designers, engineers, founders, writers, coaches, consultants and solopreneurs.

  • Quiet but alive
  • Boutique but grounded
  • Professional without corporate sterility
  • Independent without isolation

This is not a high-churn coworking warehouse. It’s not loud. It’s not transactional. It’s a small cooperative for people who appreciate calm, professionalism, and a little bit of magic outside the front door.

If you want your office to feel grounded, human, and alive — you’ll know when you walk in.

Features

Who This Is For

Studio B is for people who do real work and want a space that supports it. It’s for independent thinkers. Purpose-driven founders. Creative professionals. Writers, designers, consultants, builders.

People who value:

  • Quiet focus
  • Natural light
  • Mutual respect
  • Thoughtful conversation in the kitchen
  • A clean, beautiful shared space
